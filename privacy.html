1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to operate our business, process orders, provide customer support, manage inventory, complete shipments, process returns, and comply with legal or accounting requirements.

This information may include customer name, shipping address, billing or order details, phone number or email address when required for delivery or support, purchased item information, shipment status, tracking information, return information, and related transaction records.

2. How We Collect Information

Information may be collected through our website, customer communications, order forms, marketplace platforms, authorized APIs, shipping systems, payment or order management systems, and other tools used to process and fulfill customer orders.

When we receive information from marketplaces such as Amazon, eBay, Walmart, or other sales channels, we use that information only for the purposes allowed by the applicable marketplace policies and only as required to complete order-related business operations.

3. How We Use Information

We use personal information to process orders, confirm purchases, prepare shipments, provide tracking information, manage inventory, process returns or replacements, respond to customer service requests, prevent fraud, maintain business records, and comply with legal, tax, accounting, and marketplace requirements.

We do not use customer personal information for unrelated advertising, resale, unauthorized profiling, or any purpose not connected to our business operations or legal obligations.

4. Marketplace and Amazon Data Handling

For Amazon orders and Amazon Selling Partner API (SP-API) access, Amazon Information is collected only through authorized Amazon systems, including SP-API and Seller Central, and is used strictly for order processing, inventory management, fulfillment, shipping, customer service, returns, refunds, tax, accounting, security, and legal compliance.

Amazon Information may include buyer name, shipping address, contact details required for delivery, order number, purchased item information, shipment status, and tracking information.

We do not use Amazon Information for marketing, advertising, resale, unrelated analytics, or any purpose not permitted by Amazon policies.

5. Data Sharing

We share personal information only when necessary to complete business operations, fulfill orders, provide customer support, comply with legal obligations, or protect our business and customers.

Shipment information may be shared with shipping carriers and logistics providers strictly for delivery purposes. For Amazon-related shipments, Amazon Information is shared only when necessary with USPS, UPS, and FedEx.

The information shared with these carriers is limited to what is necessary to complete shipment and delivery, such as customer name, shipping address, contact details required by the carrier, package information, and tracking details.

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share customer personal information or Amazon Information with outside parties for marketing, advertising, or unrelated business purposes.

6. Data Storage and Security

Personal information is processed only within internal company systems and authorized service environments. Access is limited to authorized personnel who need the information to perform order fulfillment, customer support, inventory, accounting, compliance, or security duties.

Where storage is required, data is kept in secured systems with restricted access controls. We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or loss.

Sensitive data is transmitted through secure connections, including API connections and encrypted HTTPS where applicable.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to complete order processing, shipment, customer support, returns, refunds, accounting, tax, audit, fraud prevention, security, legal compliance, and marketplace compliance obligations.

We do not retain marketplace buyer data longer than necessary for permitted business purposes. When information is no longer needed, it is securely deleted, anonymized, or removed from active systems according to our internal data handling procedures.

8. Data Disposal

When data is no longer required, we dispose of it using secure deletion or anonymization methods. Paper records, if any, are securely destroyed. Electronic records are deleted from active systems unless retention is required for legal, tax, accounting, dispute resolution, fraud prevention, or compliance purposes.

9. Logs and Monitoring

System logs may be used for security, troubleshooting, and system monitoring. We do not intentionally store buyer Personally Identifiable Information (PII) in logs. If personal information appears in logs as part of an error or technical event, access to those logs is restricted and the information is handled according to our security and data disposal procedures.
