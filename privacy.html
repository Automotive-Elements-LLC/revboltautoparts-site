1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information required to process, fulfill, support, and manage customer orders. This may include buyer name, shipping address, phone number or email address when required for delivery, order number, purchased item information, shipment status, and tracking information.

Amazon Information is collected only through authorized Amazon systems, including SP-API and Seller Central, and only for legitimate business purposes related to order processing, inventory management, fulfillment, shipping, customer service, returns, refunds, tax, accounting, and legal compliance.

2. How We Use Amazon Data

Amazon Information is used strictly for business operations required to complete and support customer orders. This includes confirming orders, preparing shipments, purchasing shipping labels, providing tracking information, managing inventory, processing returns or replacements, responding to order-related customer service requests, and maintaining required business records.

We do not use Amazon Information for advertising, marketing, resale, profiling, unrelated analytics, or any purpose not permitted by Amazon policies.

3. How We Process and Store Data

Amazon Information is processed only within internal company systems and authorized service environments. Access is limited to authorized personnel who need the information to perform order fulfillment, customer support, inventory, accounting, or compliance duties.

We do not store Amazon buyer personal information longer than necessary for order fulfillment and required business purposes. Where storage is required, data is kept in secured systems with restricted access controls. We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or loss.

4. Data Sharing

Amazon Information is shared only when necessary to complete order delivery or related fulfillment services. We share limited shipment information only with USPS, UPS, and FedEx.

The information shared with these carriers is limited to what is necessary to complete shipment and delivery, such as buyer name, shipping address, contact details required by the carrier, package information, and tracking details.

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share Amazon Information with any other outside parties for marketing, advertising, or unrelated business purposes.

5. Data Retention

Amazon Information is retained only for as long as necessary to complete order processing, shipment, customer support, returns, refunds, accounting, tax, audit, fraud prevention, security, and legal compliance obligations.

When Amazon Information is no longer needed for these purposes, it is securely deleted, anonymized, or removed from active systems according to our internal data handling and disposal procedures.

6. Data Disposal

When data is no longer required, we dispose of it using secure deletion or anonymization methods. Paper records, if any, are securely destroyed. Electronic records are deleted from active systems when they are no longer needed, unless retention is required for legal, tax, accounting, dispute resolution, or compliance purposes.

7. Security Measures

We maintain reasonable security controls to protect Amazon Information and customer personal information. These controls include restricted system access, password protection, access based on business need, secure transmission methods, and monitoring of systems for unauthorized or suspicious activity.

Amazon Information is transmitted through secure connections, including API connections and encrypted HTTPS where applicable.

8. Logs and Monitoring

System logs may be used for security, troubleshooting, and system monitoring. We do not intentionally store Amazon buyer Personally Identifiable Information (PII) in logs. If any personal information appears in logs as part of an error or technical event, access to those logs is restricted and the information is handled according to our security and data disposal procedures.

10. No Sale of Personal Information

REV AND BOLT AUTO PARTS LLC does not sell customer personal information or Amazon Information. We also do not share Amazon Information for cross-context behavioral advertising or unrelated marketing purposes.

11. Incident Response

If we detect a security incident involving Amazon Information, we will investigate the issue, take steps to contain and remediate the incident, and notify required parties as applicable. If the incident involves Amazon Information, we will notify Amazon within 24 hours of detection by contacting security@amazon.com, as required by Amazon security requirements.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy and Data Handling Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our operations, legal requirements, Amazon policies, or security practices. Updates will be posted on this page.
